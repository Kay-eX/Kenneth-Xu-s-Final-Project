Bodybuilding

Bodybuilding is the use of progressive resistance exercise to control and develop one's muscles (muscle building) by muscle
hypertrophy for aesthetic purposes. It is distinct from similar activities such as powerlifting because it focuses on
physical appearance instead of strength. An individual who engages in this activity is referred to as a bodybuilder.
In professional bodybuilding, competitors appear in lineups and perform specified poses (and later individual posing routines)
for a panel of judges who rank them based on symmetry, muscularity, size, conditioning, posing, and stage presentation.
